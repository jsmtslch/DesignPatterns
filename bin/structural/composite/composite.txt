This pattern represent structure which is present with anything which has a hierarchy.
For example, an organization has an hierarchy where a manager will have many people reporting to him.
Manager himself is an employee just like the people who report to him.
But in the organization, all of them share common attributes like they have salary associated with them,
their personal details etc.
That is why all of them implement a common interface which is referred to as Component.
Then we have leaf nodes like developer.
Then we have composites like the OrgComposite
